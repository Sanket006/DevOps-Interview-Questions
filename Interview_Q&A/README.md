# 💻 Technical & Role-Based Interview Q&A

> **Company-style and role-specific** interview question sets. Unlike the tool-focused `Core_Topic_Q&A/`, these files simulate the structure and format of **actual interview rounds** at real companies and for specific roles.

---

## 📌 Purpose

This folder bridges the gap between knowing a technology and **performing in an interview**. Questions are organised by role type, interview style, and company — helping you prepare for the specific format you'll face.
