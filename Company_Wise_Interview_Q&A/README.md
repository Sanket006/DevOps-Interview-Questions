## 📌 Purpose

Generic preparation can only take you so far. This folder helps you prepare for the **specific company you are applying to** — the kinds of questions they ask, the depth they expect, and the technologies they focus on.

## 💡 How to Use

1. **Identify your target company** — if it's listed here, start with its dedicated file
2. **Check the JD analysis files** — understand what the company is actually looking for
3. **Compare with your resume** — use `Resume_&_Project_based_DevOps_Interview_Q&A/` to practice resume-specific answers
4. **For companies not listed** — use the Flytbase or TCS files as templates for the type of company (startup vs enterprise)
