# ⚡ Rapid-Fire Mock Interview Q&A

> Simulate the **speed and pressure** of a real technical interview — answer quickly, clearly, and confidently.

---

## 📌 Purpose

Rapid-fire rounds are common in DevOps interviews, especially at the start of a technical screen. Interviewers ask short, direct questions and expect **concise, confident answers within 30–60 seconds each**.

This folder trains exactly that skill.

---

## ⏱️ How to Use

**Option 1 — Solo Practice**
1. Set a 60-second timer per question
2. Answer out loud before reading the provided answer
3. Mark questions you struggled with and revisit them

**Option 2 — Mock Interview with a Peer**
1. One person reads the question, the other answers
2. Switch roles after 15 questions
3. Grade each other on clarity and completeness

**Option 3 — Pre-Interview Warm-Up**
- Run through 20 questions the morning of your interview to activate recall
